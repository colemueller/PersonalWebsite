ABOUT

I graduated from Drexel University in 2018 with a degree in Game Art and Production and a minor in Computer Science. I'm currently with Gromelski & Associates, Inc. as a multimedia developer programmer.

When I'm not working, I spend my time at the climbing gym, hiking, reading comics, or watching movies. I'd love to discuss projects that I might be a good fit for, so please feel free to contact me to chat!

K'NEX® 3D

Spring/summer - 2017

K'NEX® 3D was one of my primary projects while I was with K'NEX®. I maintained current product lines and added new products to the app. I also added developer features to streamline the addition and testing of new products in the app without needing to build and publish a new version.

I also developed a WebGL version of the app to be embedded directly on the K'NEX® website that navigated the user directly to a specific product in the app based on the product page being viewed. I developed JavaScript features to communicate with the WEbGL app and customize the Unity WebGL player.

Tools:

  • Unity 3D
  • Autodesk Maya
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Android / iOS development tools
  • Google Play, Apple App Store, and Amazon Appstore developer tools

GROUNDLESS

winter 2017 - Fall 2018

"Groundless" is a local multiplayer arena game with no ground! Players compete by building and destroying the land they walk on.

"Groundless" was my senior capstone project and was shown at PAX East in 2018. I worked as one of the lead programmers and game designers. I designed and implemented core gameplay mechanics, as well as many of the effects and shaders. I also implemented many of the backend systems such as local 4-player input, menu navigation, and character animation.

"Groundless" is available on Steam, XBOX ONE, and the Windows 10 Store.

Tools:

  • Unity 3D
  • C#
  • Autodesk Maya
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Audiokinetic Wwise

BEBRIS

spring 2018

"Bebris" is an arcade spelling game where players spell words using the letters falling down the screen to score points! Download "Bebris" from my Itch.io page below.

This was a short-term Unity project completed with two other team members. I developed the grid system that controlled letter spawning, placement, movement, and gravity. I also implemented player input, UI, animation, and effects.

Tools:

  • Unity 2D
  • C#

MOCEAN

fall 2017

"Mocean" was a ~3 week project done by myself and 2 team members. This project was a study in graphics programming and technical art implementation methods.

This project utilizes GPU instancing, cloth physics, procedural mesh generation, procedural animation through a vertex shader, and more.

Tools:

  • Unity 3D
  • C#
  • Autodesk Maya

DRIPPY

in progress

"Drippy" is an in progress mobile game project I'm slowly developing in my free time. "Drippy" is an infinite scroller game where players must fall as far as they can while avoiding being flung off the screen by their own tap or one of the many obstacles that they'll find along their fall.

I developed gameplay and UI interaction as well as the many of the game systems such as local high scores, player settings, and animation systems. I also created all of the game art and animation in Procreate - a first for me!

Tools:

  • Unity 2D
  • C#
  • Procreate
