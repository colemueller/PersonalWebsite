PRIVACY POLICY

Data Collected

If the game crashes, anonymous data is sent to Unity. I use this data to diagnose issues and work on fixes. No identifiable data is recieved or kept. My games do not request special permissions or access to personal data of any kind.

Note that your data will be stored by Unity.

Data Sharing

User data is collected by a third party (Unity). Please refer to their Privacy Policy. I will not share user data to any third party unless required to do so by law.
